Key Commands:

    Texture Mapping Controls:

     F1 - toggle ambient occlusion
     F2 - toggle color
     F3 - toggle both

    Perspective Viewing Controls:

     Q - Increase camera position in x-axis
     W - Increase camera position in y-axis
     E - Increase camera position in z-axis
     R - Zoom in
     A - Decrease camera position in x-axis
     S - Decrease camera position in y-axis
     D - Decrease camera position in z-axis
     F - Zoom out
     I - Increase in x-axis of lookat position
     O - Increase in y-axis of lookat position
     P - Increase in z-axis of lookat position
     J - Decrease in x-axis of lookat position
     K - Decrease in y-axis of lookat position
     L - Decrease in z-axis of lookat position
     CAPS LOCK - toggle upsideDown     

    Object Controls:

     KEYPAD_7 - Rotate CCW in x
     KEYPAD_9 - Rotate CW in x
     KEYPAD_4 - Rotate CCW in y
     KEYPAD_6 - Rotate CW in y
     KEYPAD_1 - Rotate CCW in z
     KEYPAD_3 - Rotate CW in z
     KEYPAD_2 - Rotate CW in all axis
     KEYPAD_5 - Rotate CCW in all axis
     KEYPAD_ADD - Scale up
     KEYPAD_SUBTRACT - Scale down
     UP - Move up
     DOWN - Move down
     LEFT - Move left
     RIGHT - Move right

    Light Positioning Controls:
     
     INSERT - Increase x coord of light position
     HOME - Increase y coord of light position
     PG_UP - Increase z coord of light position
     DELETE - Decrease x coord of light position
     END - Decrease y coord of light position
     PG_DOWN - Decrease z coord of light position

    Window Controls:

     ESC - Close window
     SPACE - Reset to default state
